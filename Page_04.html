Overview of the Interview

This video features an exclusive interview with actor Colin Morgan regarding the conclusion of the hit BBC fantasy series, 'Merlin'. The discussion emphasizes behind-the-scenes insights, the emotional weight of saying goodbye to the show, and personal reflections on a five-year artistic journey.

The Concept & Core Topics Discussed

1. Series Conclusion

Colin Morgan reflects on the five-year journey of the show, expressing his belief that it has reached its natural, strongest point to end rather than continuing unnecessarily.

2. Character Evolution

Discussion on how the characters matured over time. Morgan details how his portrayal of 'Merlin' became more focused and ruthless in the final series due to the heavy weight of destiny and impending doom.

3. On-Set Memories

Morgan shares fond memories of his first day on set working with Richard Wilson, highlighting how that initial professional relationship grew into a lasting friendship.

4. Gratitude

The interview concludes with a heartfelt thank you from Morgan to the fans for their overwhelming, loyal support and encouragement throughout the series run.

Reflective Growth

Throughout the interview, Colin Morgan reflects on the 'reflective growth' and deeper evolution of the 'Merlin' series over its landmark five-year journey.

  • Natural Evolution Morgan describes the show as having run its 'natural course', having grown and darkened each year, ultimately reaching a point where the creators felt it was right to conclude at its 'strongest point'.
  • Character Maturity He notes that both the characters and the audience have 'grown up' alongside the series. Reflecting on his own role, he shares how 'Merlin' evolved from early days where he felt like he had 'no idea' what he was doing on his first day to becoming a more 'ruthless,' 'focused,' and 'adult' character in the final series.
  • The Weight of Destiny He explains that the character of 'Merlin' has gained a deeper understanding of fate. He reflects on how 'Merlin' learns to accept that some things are out of his control, transforming from a position of trying to fight destiny to realizing this may be exactly how things were meant to be.
  • Professional Relationships Morgan speaks to the growth of his bond with co-star 'Richard Wilson'. What began as a nervous first encounter on set blossomed into a lasting, 'brilliant friendship,' illustrating how the professional environment fostered personal development.
